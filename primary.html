SEKOLAH KEBANGSAAN PEKAN DUA

After graduating from kindergarten at St. Anne Kindy School, I continued my educational journey at Sekolah Kebangsaan Pekan Dua in 2013. It was there that I first learned how to communicate confidently, as I had been known as a very shy girl. Every year, I participated in storytelling competitions and consistently achieved at least a top-three placing. These experiences helped me develop my public speaking skills and self-confidence. Therefore, I am forever grateful to my primary school teachers for their guidance, encouragement, and support in helping me become more confident in expressing myself. A special shout-out to Mr. Din, my English teacher that had polished me into speaking English more fluently.
